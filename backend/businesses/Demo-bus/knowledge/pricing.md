# Flowrep - AI Receptionist Service

## About Flowrep
Flowrep is an AI-powered receptionist service that handles phone calls for businesses 24/7. Our AI receptionists can answer questions, book appointments, transfer calls, and provide information to callers.

1. Sign up for a plan
2. We configure your AI receptionist with your business information
3. Forward your phone number to our system
4. Your AI receptionist starts handling calls immediately

## Common Questions

**Q: How quickly can I get started?**
A: You can be up and running in less than 24 hours after signing up.

**Q: Can I change plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
